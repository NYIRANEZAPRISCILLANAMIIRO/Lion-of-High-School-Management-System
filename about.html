Lion of Judah High School was founded in 2017 by the school Director Pr.Saul Yawe with an aim of providing accessible and quality education to students in the community. Over the years, it has grown into a respected institution known for Academic performance, God-fearing and discipline.

This school focuses on academic excellence, discipline and character development to prepare learners for higher education and future careers.
